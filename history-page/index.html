ABOUT THE COLLEGE

AKCAS blossomed on the education map of Srivilliputtur Taluk in 1998, which is glitter like a beacon of student community in the rural area at Krishnankoil. The college is located among the scenic beauty of the Western Ghats with its gorgeous pillars touching the sky. The committed visionary and dedicated philanthropist ‘Kalvivallal’ Thiru T. Kalasalingam founded this college. It was the grand vision of the founding father that those who enter this citadel of learning would leave not only as distinguished degree holders but also as men and women of characters. The honourable members of the management, teaching and non-teaching staff have been striving towards that goal.

VISION

  • To impart avowedly quality education at affordable cost to the students of the less privileged to marshal into an era of excellence, innovation and employability.

MISSION

  • To produce socially committed citizens of integrity with qualities of self- confidence, perseverance, patriotism and respect for the fellow human beings.

QUALITY OBJECTIVES

  • To cultivate academic excellence and communicative competence of the rural background students.
  • To impart Human values and Skills to become successful and adorable.
  • To sustain and develop the identity of a student to high caliber.
  • To inculcate in the students a sense of achievement of rich spiritual heritage and the higher faculties of the mind.
  • To inspire students to develop personality traits and become responsible citizens.

LIBRARY

AKCAS Library offers a wealth of resources and services to support the teaching and research activities of the campus. It provides access to reliable information and expert services that support student and lifelong learning in a welcoming environment. Both the teaching faculty and the library faculty participate in collection development for the library, whose is now at approximately ___ volumes. The library subscribes to more than _____ serial titles, including magazines and journals and several state, national and international newspapers. It serves as a knowledge hub containing resources not only in the form of print but also in e- versions and provides students access to a vast repository of resources, including books and journals both print and online. In our institution AKCAS Library plays a vital role in the dissemination of knowledge.

LANGUAGE LAB

Our AKCAS has a with all mod cons Language Lab with _____ computers installed with all the accessories such as head-phones, speakers and software packages. The lab is bequeathed with sophisticated machines of Language Lab system required for equipping oneself for undertaking GRE, TOEFL, IELTS, etc., exams. It has a great role in imparting skills of language in very novel way. It makes the production of requisite pedagogical materials imperative, particularly in the case of teaching of language.

SPORTS

The college aims at the holistic development of each student and the Sports Club has an important role in this. AKCAS Sports Club can be proud of its endeavours in producing the students with a cemented basketball court, lawn tennis court, volleyball court, shuttle badminton court, cricket court, athletic field and gymnasium facilities. The college also provides indoor facilities for table tennis, caroms and chess. We feel happy and fulfilled that our students make the best use of the facilities provided by winning several competitions held.

DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER (B.Sc CS, IT & BCA)

The mission of the Department of Computer Science is to persistently strive for achieving excellence in computing disciplines. It is being pursued through its spectrum of academic programmes in computing of contemporary standards. The sustained effort is to produce computing graduates with potential to design and develop systems involving the integration of software and hardware devices; innovative approaches to programming and problem solving as well as creative ways to use computers, large scale software systems and computing infrastructure of any organization.

DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE

The Commerce curriculum is extremely comprehensive touching upon every aspect of business and management. It covers all the bases and equips students with the necessary knowledge and skill. It incorporates a wide array of subjects that include Accountancy, Mathematics, Business and Company Law, Human Resource Management, Marketing, Economics, Finance, Banking, Income Tax and Entrepreneurship. Our academic program is of the highest caliber because we believe in the transformative power of ideas. Our faculty, and diverse student body from various social walks of life, all contribute towards the same purpose: to maintain the department’s excellent reputation in education at the cutting edge of economic, institutional and organizational aspects of commerce.

DEPARTMENT OF PHYSICS

The objective of Physics Department is to impart adequate fundamental knowledge of Physics to the graduates so that the students have a basis for complete understanding of current and future scientific technological developments. The department provides good infrastructure to its students. The labs are well outfitted with modern sophisticated equipments as per the curriculum. Highly qualified faculties make sure about the good pedagogy.

DEPARTMENT OF BIO-TECHNOLOGY

Bio-Technology department is surpassing in teaching and research in the thrust areas of Bio-technology and related fields. It provides spacious lab with all modern facilities which can induce the young minds to shape their career. It produces technically sound, practical, self-sustained Bio-technologist to meet the challenging technological environment. It advances knowledge and educates students in Bio- technology that will best serve the Nation and the World.

DEPARTMENT OF ENGLISH

The English Department provides students with tailor-made curriculum to enhance their language competencies. It is devoted to the critical study of literature and language, indeed to the study of creative expression of many kinds, including film and other media. It offers courses in all periods and genres of English, American and Anglophone literature – poetry, fiction, non-fiction, and drama – as well as a wide array of classes in creative writing, film, media studios, journalism, rhetoric and theater. It joins critics, scholars, and artists in an environment that fosters interactive learning and teaching, with extensive opportunities to pursue internships and independent research.

DEPARTMENT OF MATHEMATICS

The Mathematics Department fosters an academic climate of scholarship and learning. The student is aided in developing quantitative skills and ability to think clearly and critically about complex problems while communicating results with precision. The Department will foster within each student an aesthetic appreciation for mathematical thinking. It should apprise students of the vigorous expectations of college level mathematics and the available resources to assist in their learning.

EXTRA CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES

Mere classroom teaching is inefficient to bring about all round development of student is a major part of our perspective planning. In the global world of today students with manifold talents is in great demand in all walks of life. To reflect our goals and missions we established various associations to organize various Co- curricular and Extra-Curricular activities. These associations have given significant result over the years. Competitions, speeches of eminent persons, and other activities related to the student quality are undertaken through out the year.
